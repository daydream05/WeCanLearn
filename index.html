Web Development

  1. Free Code Camp

By far the best FREE web development curriculum out there. Like most online programming tutorials, Free Code Camp holds your hand until you have a basic understanding of the concepts. (i.e. look at this code, type this code). However, what separates this website from others is that once you get your feet wet, you're on your own. It goes beyond repetitive console input/output. It actually gives you real life projects to work on. If you get stuck(I did and some people did), Free Code Camp has a really helpful community to help you out. Once you are done with all the projects for the specific certification you can move on to the next. Free Code Camp consists of 4 certifications:

  • Front End Development: Html/CSS/Javascript
  • Data Visualization: Sass/React/D3
  • Back End Development MongoDB/Express/Node
  • Full Stack Development

If you are able to get the first three certifications then you qualify to take the Full Stack development certification which lets you build websites for non-profit organizations! Most people who has followed the curriculum religiously, however, end up not finishing the whole thing because they get hired right away. (Tons of challenging projects to show). I didn't finish it either because I got lazy. So don't be like me!
